Milo Buns

Take a bite of these soft buns and out comes our luscious malted chocolate filling made from Milo! Baked until golden brown and covered in a Milo crust, you'll be having these Milo Buns everyday.

100% Healthy

Milo Buns are a delicious and nutritious bread variety made with a unique blend of ingredients. These buns are crafted using a combination of whole wheat flour, millet, and a touch of natural sweeteners like honey or maple syrup. They are rich in fiber, vitamins, and minerals, making them a healthy choice for bread lovers.

Best Seller

Milo Buns have quickly become a best-seller in the bread market, captivating the hearts and taste buds of health-conscious individuals everywhere. These buns are crafted with utmost care, using a secret recipe that combines the goodness of whole wheat flour, ancient grains, and a hint of natural sweetness.
